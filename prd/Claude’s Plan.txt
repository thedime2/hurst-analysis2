Context
Two reproduction tasks from Hurst's Profit Magic

Page 45 Figures II-9 (Time-Persistence of Cyclicality) and II-10 (Principle of Variation at Work) — a single bandpass filter applied to DJIA, shown without and with amplitude envelopes. We compare modulate vs subtract bandpass construction to validate envelope extraction.
